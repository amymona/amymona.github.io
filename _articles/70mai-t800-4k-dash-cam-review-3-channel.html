Frequently Asked Questions

Does the T800 include the memory card?

Yes! The memory card comes packaged with the dash cam in the same box. Many users miss it initially, but it's definitely included.

What's the difference between T800-2 and T800-41 models?

The T800-41 includes a 4K rear camera (RC41) with Sony IMX678 sensor, while the T800-2 includes a 1080P rear camera (RC24) with Sony IMX662 sensor. Choose based on your need for rear camera resolution.

Can I use the parking mode without hardwiring?

No, the parking surveillance features require the 70mai Hardwire Kit UP04 for continuous power while the vehicle is off. This is sold separately.

How difficult is the installation process?

Most users complete installation in 30-60 minutes. The included 3.5M cables provide plenty of length for most vehicles. Professional installation is recommended if you're adding the hardwire kit.

Does the 4G functionality work worldwide?

The 4G compatibility supports most networks, but you'll need to purchase the separate 4G module and ensure compatibility with your local cellular providers.

How long does footage stay on the memory card?

With loop recording and a 512GB card, you can store approximately 2-3 weeks of continuous driving footage before it begins overwriting older files.

Is the voice control responsive while driving?

Yes, the voice recognition works well even with road noise. I tested it with windows down and music playing, and it consistently recognized commands.
